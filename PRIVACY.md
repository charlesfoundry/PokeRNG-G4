# Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

PokeRNG G4 is an offline RNG utility for Generation 4 Pokemon games. The app is
designed to perform calculations on the device.

## Data Collection

PokeRNG G4 does not collect, transmit, sell, rent, or share personal data.

The app does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, third-party tracking, or
remote crash reporting.

## Data Stored on Your Device

PokeRNG G4 may store app settings locally on your device, including:

- selected language and game version
- TID/SID values entered by the user
- timer calibration values
- saved RNG targets
- egg parent IV settings and egg RNG settings

This data is stored only on your device and is not sent to the developer or to
any external server by PokeRNG G4.

## In-App Purchases

PokeRNG G4 may offer optional "Support developer" in-app purchases. These
purchases do not unlock additional app features.

In-app purchases are processed by Apple through App Store services. PokeRNG G4
does not receive or store your payment card information, billing address, or
other payment credentials.

## Network Access

The app does not require a user account and does not upload RNG data.

Some system services, such as App Store in-app purchases, may communicate with
Apple when you choose to use them. Links shown in the About section, such as the
project page or this privacy policy, may open outside the app if you choose to
open them.

## Data Deletion

Because PokeRNG G4 stores its user data locally, deleting the app from your
device removes the app's locally stored data.

## Children

PokeRNG G4 does not knowingly collect data from children or from any other user.

## Contact

For privacy questions, use the developer contact information provided on the
App Store product page.
