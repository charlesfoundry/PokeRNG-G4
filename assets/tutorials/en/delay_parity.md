# Switching Delay Parity

## When to Switch

If the target Delay and your actual hit Delay have different parity, the current DS year cannot hit that Delay.

For example:

- The target Delay is even, but your hits are always odd.
- The target Delay is odd, but your hits are always even.

This is not an app filter problem.

## How to Switch

Do not change any app search filters.

Do not change the year used by the app search.

Only change the DS system year by **+1** or **-1**.

Changing the DS year switches Delay parity.

## Notes

Keep the year in the app the same as the year used when searching the target.

Only change the year on the DS system to switch parity during the real attempt.

After switching, keep using the same target time, Delay, and timer flow.
