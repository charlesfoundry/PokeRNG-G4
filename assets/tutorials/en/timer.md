# DP/Pt/HGSS Timer Input Timing

## Core Rule

At the end of each timer stage, the app plays **4 short beeps**.

The correct input timing is **the exact moment the 4th beep starts**.

## How to Press

1. Listen to the first 3 beeps.
2. Use their rhythm to predict when the 4th beep begins.
3. Press A at the exact moment the 4th beep starts.

The A press may also be another target action, such as:

- starting the game
- entering the save
- triggering an encounter
- pressing A on a stationary Pokemon

## Note

If you wait until you hear the 4th beep and then press, you are already late.

In this app, the moment the 4th beep starts is the exact countdown end time for that stage.
